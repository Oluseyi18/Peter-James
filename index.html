About Me

I'm Peter James and Photography is my world!

I am a lover of the outdoors and I'm always looking for new adventures, sharing my latest work mainly on my Instagram page and other media platforms. For me, every picture is worth a thousand words- so it's all about capturing the moment, and telling your story to the world.

Some of my most popular photographs include landscape, portrait, and product photography. You can find me on Instagram at @pteerjames.. I am an experienced all round photographer and I'm based in Lagos, Nigeria. Check out my services below.
